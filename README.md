# General Idea
Count Down is depressed because he's broke. He needs money (and blood?).
Venturing out from his lair in Victorian (?) england he robs (and feeds on) people to take their money back to his lair.

# Specific Ideas
Walk through the town to find lonely civilians (or cops). Attack a civilian without being seen and take their money. With their big bags of money make your way back to your lair without being seen. If a civilian sees you attacking someone or with illgotten gain, they will scream and attract the cops. Cops walk around with a flashlight / lantern, the light from the lantern will scatter you into bats, only to reform in your coffin (without the cash you need).

Countdown till dawn.
